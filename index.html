Welcome to AnimalShelter!

AnimalShelter is a charity organization that promotes animal care by spaying/neutering stray dogs and cats, providing shelter to animals that are injured or in danger and informing citizens on how they can help support animal welfare. We have been active since 2014 and our community keeps on growing. Join our cause!
